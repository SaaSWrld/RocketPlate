FAQ

Good questions.

Is this just another delivery app?
No. There's no marketplace, no per-order fees, no tipping. You subscribe to a weekly plan, restaurants batch-cook it fresh, and we run one nightly route so it arrives hot. One flat price, everything included.
Where is RocketPlate launching first?
Miami-Dade, starting around Kendall / Pinecrest (ZIP 33186). We open neighborhood by neighborhood so routes stay tight and food stays hot. Expansion order is decided by waitlist demand.
My ZIP isn't in the pilot. Should I still join?
Yes — every signup literally moves your ZIP up the launch map. The neighborhoods with the most demand open next.
Can I change or skip meals?
Swap tomorrow's meal until 9 PM the night before, add same-day items until 1 PM, and skip or pause any week. One decision a week, fully in your control.
I run a restaurant — how do I get involved?
RocketPlate turns your slow afternoon hours into predictable, pre-counted revenue with no dine-in disruption. Apply as a partner kitchen →
