Questions, answered

Three audiences. Straight answers.

For subscribers

Is this just another delivery app?
Nope — it's almost the opposite. Delivery apps are on-demand: per-order fees, tip screens, surge pricing, and food that waits on multi-stop routes. RocketPlate is a weekly subscription. Neighborhood kitchens cook your meals fresh, one dedicated route brings them hot in a set evening window, and your flat weekly price already includes everything.
Can I change or skip meals?
Swap tomorrow's meal until 9 PM tonight, add same-day extras until 1 PM, and skip weeks or pause anytime. One decision a week, adjustable when life happens.
Are there delivery fees or tips?
No delivery fees, no service fees, no tips — ever. The weekly price covers the food, the route, and the driver's pay.

For restaurants

How does RocketPlate fit into my kitchen day?
You get final counts by 10 PM the night before and cook during your quiet 2–4 PM window. One batch, one pickup — no tablets chirping through dinner service, no drivers at your counter.
What does it cost to join?
Nothing to apply. RocketPlate handles subscriptions, payments, packaging standards, routing, delivery, and customer care — you cook, we pay you for prepaid batch volume.
How do I apply?
Use the waitlist form and choose "Restaurant" — our partnerships team reaches out with the founding kitchen details and the five-step onboarding path.

About the launch

Where is RocketPlate launching first?
Miami-Dade, starting around Kendall / Pinecrest (ZIP 33186). We launch neighborhood by neighborhood so routes stay tight and food stays hot.
My ZIP isn't in the pilot. Should I still join?
Yes — expansion order is decided by waitlist demand. Every signup literally moves your ZIP up the launch map.

The first hundred subscribers in each launch zone: first week free, 33% off month two, a 12-month rate lock, and early access before public launch. Spots go in waitlist order.
