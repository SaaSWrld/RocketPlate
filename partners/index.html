You already have the kitchen. Use the quiet hours.

Most independent kitchens run hot at lunch and dinner and idle in between. That afternoon valley is staffed, equipped, and paid for — but producing little. RocketPlate turns that window into planned, pre-counted production: a controlled batch channel, not another marketplace fighting for your dinner rush.

Why batch beats chaos

A production channel, not a scramble.

Marketplace delivery is unpredictable by design. Batch production is the opposite — everything is known before you turn on the burners.

Pre-counted demand
