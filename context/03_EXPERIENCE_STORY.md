# 03 — EXPERIENCE STORY (canon)

Every journey is a mission with MO as flight attendant and Rocky as the
vehicle. The user is **crew, not customer**.

## Subscriber arc
1. **Arrival** — cinematic opening (or hero film) establishes the world in
   seconds: hot local meals, one weekly subscription, zero delivery chaos.
2. **Briefing** — the problem (checkout-shock receipt) and the model (3 moving
   parts) told as mission telemetry inside warm cards.
3. **Confidence** — real kitchens (representative dishes until partners are
   public), real ZIP demand counts, honest launch status. Never a fake number.
4. **Commitment** — "Join the Launch Crew": light capture (4 fields + consents),
   progress bar as fueling gauge. Duplicates hear the truth ("already on the
   launch list — preferences updated").
5. **Celebration** — success = a launch: mini liftoff animation, zone status,
   referral link ("move your ZIP up the launch map"), account magic link.
   MO's voice: warm, brief, delighted — display language, 2–3 words (Character Bible). Motif allowed here.
6. **Return** — /account is the crew locker: preferences, referral count, zone.
   Emails land as Mission Control transmissions (worker templates).

## Restaurant arc
Own a kitchen → /partners: the dead-zone economics story → 15-field application →
tokenized status = a launch pipeline (Applied → Menu fit → Tasting → Menu setup →
Go-live). Ops-credible tone; mono telemetry for counts and windows.

## Operator arc (Andre)
/admin is Mission Control proper: real aggregates only, CSV exports, partner
pipeline. The demand signal decides the launch map — the product's core loop.

## Emotional rules
Delight punctuates, never obstructs (skippable, fast, reduced-motion-safe).
Warmth over cleverness when they conflict. Every promise on screen must be
literally true today.
