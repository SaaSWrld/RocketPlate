Questions, answered

Before you ask.

Is this just another delivery app?
Nope — it's almost the opposite. Delivery apps are on-demand: per-order fees, tip screens, surge pricing, and food that waits on multi-stop routes. RocketPlate is a weekly subscription. Neighborhood kitchens cook your meals fresh, one dedicated route brings them hot in a set evening window, and your flat weekly price already includes everything.
Are the meals actually hot?
Yes — that's the whole design. Meals are cooked the same afternoon, leave the kitchen hot in insulated batch carriers, and ride one direct route in your zone. They're never rewarmed, never parked outside another customer's house.
When do deliveries happen?
One evening window: 4:15–6:30 PM, right at dinnertime. You'll know your zone's typical arrival time after your first week.
What areas are launching first?
Select Miami-Dade neighborhoods, starting around Kendall / Pinecrest (ZIP 33186). Launch zones are prioritized by waitlist demand — check your ZIP above to see where you stand, and share with neighbors to move your area up.
Can I change my meals?
Yes. Swap tomorrow's meal until 9 PM tonight, add same-day extras until 1 PM, and skip weeks or pause anytime. One decision a week, adjustable whenever life happens.
Are there delivery fees or tips?
No delivery fees. No service fees. No tips — ever. The weekly price you see includes the food, the delivery, and the driver's pay. That's the point.
What is the Founding 100?
The first hundred subscribers in each launch zone. They get their first week free, 33% off month two, a 12-month rate lock, and early access before public launch. Spots go in waitlist order.
Can restaurants apply?
Absolutely — independent local kitchens are the heart of RocketPlate. Join the waitlist and pick "Restaurant Owner," and our partnerships team will reach out with the founding kitchen details.
