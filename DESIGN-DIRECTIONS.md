# RocketPlate — Cinematic Design Directions

Four directions explored before the final build; approved hybrid at the end.
(Approved by Andre, 2026-07-07.)

## D1 — "Launch Dinner" (brand-iconic)
- **Hero:** rocket on a steam-rolled launch pad, T-minus energy; scroll = liftoff, rocket flies the whole page.
- **World:** cream sky, warm orange/crimson food-light, navy launch-night, filmic grain. Kitchen steam, never industrial smoke.
- **Motion:** scroll-throttled flight path keyframed per section; velocity stretches the flame; word-staggered headlines.
- **3D:** one procedural WebGL rocket + orbiting plate payload; everything else CSS/GSAP.
- **Type:** Fraunces display / Outfit body / Plex Mono telemetry.
- **Pacing:** ignition → telemetry ticker → problem → model → offer → partners → payload deploy (form) → drift-away.
- **CTA:** crimson pills, shine sweep, boarding language.
- **Fits because:** most ownable; makes a pre-launch company feel like an event; mascot scales to packaging/trucks/social.
- **Risks:** cartoonish if overplayed; WebGL battery cost; metaphor can crowd out appetite.

## D2 — "The Daily Meal Orbit" (systemic)
- **Hero:** cuisine cards/plans orbit a central plate-rocket; the subscription as a solar system.
- **World:** cleaner, diagrammatic; thin orbital rings; color from food cards.
- **Motion:** slow rotation, pause on hover; scroll advances station-to-station (kitchen → batch → route → table); scroll-drawn path lines.
- **3D:** 2.5D — CSS orbits w/ counter-rotated cards, SVG paths.
- **Type:** editorial-systematic; mono as orbital designations.
- **Pacing:** each station explains one mechanism.
- **CTA:** "join the rotation"; form docks as final satellite.
- **Fits because:** RocketPlate's invention IS a system; best scaffold for weekly rhythm.
- **Risks:** weak emotional hook; gimmicky-SaaS risk; better as a section than a whole site.

## D3 — "Mission Control Miami" (operational)
- **Hero:** premium launch dashboard — Miami-Dade map, pilot beacon, demand indicators, ZIP console.
- **World:** navy-dominant panels, cream text, orange/crimson status lights, mono everywhere.
- **Motion:** data choreography — real Supabase aggregates only, pulsing beacons, pipeline stages lighting in sequence.
- **3D:** none; SVG maps, animated strokes, radar rings; rocket as glyph only.
- **Type:** mono-forward, tabular numbers; serif reserved for human moments.
- **Pacing:** status → zones → demand → pipeline → apply/join.
- **CTA:** console actions (CHECK MY ZIP, ENTER PARTNER PIPELINE).
- **Fits because:** projects operational competence to investors/operators; makes the waitlist a visible flywheel.
- **Risks:** cold for consumers; dark UI fights warm palette; small real numbers undercut the story early (show status, not counts, until volume).

## D4 — "Neighborhood Supper Club of the Future" (emotional)
- **Hero:** full-bleed dish cinematography, curling steam, minimal type; rocket as a maker's-mark accent.
- **World:** deepest warmth — cream/terracotta, large food photography, evening golden light, Miami neighborhood identity.
- **Motion:** slow, generous; long crossfades, steam drift; the page breathes.
- **3D:** none required; photography/video does depth.
- **Type:** Fraunces at its most expressive, menu-like; storytelling body copy.
- **Pacing:** feeling → who cooks → how the week works → join the neighborhood.
- **CTA:** RSVP-soft ("Save my seat").
- **Fits because:** sells dinner itself to the person who pays; ages past launch phase.
- **Risks:** collapses without real food imagery; least differentiated; underuses the rocket.

## ✅ Approved hybrid — "Warm Launch"
| Layer | Source | Concretely |
|---|---|---|
| Hero + page spine | D1 | rocket hero (video slot + WebGL placeholder), launch pacing, throttled flame, ticker |
| Storytelling core | D2 | cuisine orbit, kitchen→route→door system framing, plans as satellites |
| Waitlist/ZIP machinery | D3 | navy console ZIP module + launch map, mono telemetry, real aggregates only |
| Temperature | D4 | cream-dominant surfaces, steam motifs, food imagery slots, RSVP-warm subscriber copy |

**Coherence rule:** mission-control navy lives ONLY inside contained cards (ZIP console,
map, dead-zone chart, ticker) sitting on the warm cream world — a supper club that runs
like mission control, never the reverse. Mono labels operational facts only (times, ZIPs,
counts); the serif owns everything human.

**Hero film art direction (Higgsfield/Magica pipeline):** Warm Launch — locked back-view
still, hull text "YOUR FAVORITE MEALS DELIVERED DAILY", kitchen steam + golden-hour food
light; rotate to front; robot waves from porthole (left hand) holding serving dish (right
hand); flies off. Not cold aerospace.
